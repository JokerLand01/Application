Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Streakle ("we", "the game") is a free daily word puzzle. This page explains what data the game uses and how. We keep things deliberately minimal.

Information we store

Streakle does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, or any personal information. To make the game work, we store the following on your own device using your browser's local storage:

  • Your progress on the current day's puzzle.
  • Your statistics (games played, win percentage, current and max streak, guess distribution).
  • Your theme preference (light or dark).

This information never leaves your device and is not sent to us. You can erase it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Cookies and advertising

If advertising is enabled, Streakle may use third-party vendors, including Google, which use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising at aboutads.info/choices.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to count visits and understand which features are used, in aggregate. This does not identify you personally.

Children

Streakle is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
