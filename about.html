About Streakle

Streakle is a free daily word puzzle. Every day there's one new five-letter word, the same for everyone, and your job is to figure it out in six guesses or fewer.

How to play

Type any real five-letter word and press Enter. The tiles change color to give you clues:

  • Green — the letter is correct and in the right spot.
  • Yellow — the letter is in the word but in a different spot.
  • Gray — the letter isn't in the word.

Use the clues to narrow it down. Solve it and you keep your streak going — come back tomorrow for a new word and a longer streak.

Want to solve it faster? Read our strategy guide and tips for the best starting words and a step-by-step method.

Why people love it

It's quick (a couple of minutes a day), it's the same puzzle for everyone so you can compare scores, and when you finish you get a spoiler-free emoji grid you can share with friends — no spoilers, just bragging rights.

Free to play

Streakle is free. It's supported by unobtrusive advertising so we can keep releasing a fresh puzzle every single day.
